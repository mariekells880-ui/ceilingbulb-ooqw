​Privacy Policy

1. What This Policy Is All About

​Your privacy matters to us, and we promise to protect your personal info by following all the rules about privacy and data safety.

2. The Info We Grab

We might collect a few kinds of info:

a. Personal Info

This is info that can point to you, like:

Your full name

Your email address

Your phone number

Where to send stuff and where to bill you

How you pay (but that goes through secure payment companies)

Your login info

b. Usage Data

This is what we get automatically when you use our stuff, like:

Your IP address

What kind of browser you’re using

What kind of device you’re using

What operating system you’re using

When you visited our website

What pages you checked out

c. Cookies and Stuff Like That

We use cookies and other tech to make your experience better and see how you use our Services.

3. How We Use Your Info

We might use the info we collect to:

Give you our Services and keep them running

Handle your orders

Talk to you (like with updates, help, or deals)

Make our website, products, and services better

Find and fix problems or stop fraud

Follow the law

4. Legal Stuff About Your Data (For Europe)

If you’re in Europe, here’s why we can use your data:

You said it was okay

We need it to make our agreement work

The law says we have to

We have a valid business reason

5. Sharing Your Info

We don’t sell or rent your personal info.

We might share your info with:

Companies that help us do things (like payment processors, hosting, and data analysis)

The authorities, if the law says we have to or to protect our rights

Other businesses if we merge, buy, or sell the company

Everyone we share with has to keep your data safe and follow this Privacy Policy.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We only keep your personal data as long as we need it for what we said in this Privacy Policy, unless the law says we have to keep it longer.

7. How We Protect Your Data

We use safety measures to protect your personal info from being seen, changed, stolen, or destroyed by anyone who shouldn’t.

But keep in mind, nothing on the internet is totally secure.

8. Where Your Data Goes

If you’re using our Services from outside, your info might go to other countries where the data protection laws might be different.

We’ll take steps to make sure your data is protected according to this Privacy Policy.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you can:

See your data and get a copy of it

Ask us to fix or delete your data

Take back your permission

Say no to us using your data or ask us to limit how we use it

Ask us to move your data somewhere else


10. Kids' Privacy

Our Services are for adults and teens.

We don’t knowingly collect info from young kids.

If we find out we have a kid’s info, we’ll delete it ASAP.

11. Changes to This Policy

We might change this Privacy Policy sometimes.

If we do, we’ll post the changes on this page with the new “Last Updated” date.

Check back here often to stay in the loop.
